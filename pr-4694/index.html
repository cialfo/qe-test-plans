Regression
Discovery & Research — University Profile TC-UPROF-029–030 Fit Overview Tuition card (buildTuitionCard/getAnnualTuitionText) now prefers tuition_fee_range (local currency) over tuition_fee_range_usd and renders the university's real currency code instead of a hardcoded 'USD' suffix. Both TCs assume USD-only display and need re-verification against universities with local-currency tuition data.
Discovery & Research — University Profile TC-UPROF-068, TC-UPROF-070, TC-UPROF-106 BUDGET_MAX_VALUES and the currency-symbol map were extracted out of UniversityCostComponent into the shared constants.ts (now also consumed by FitOverviewComponent). Pure refactor with no logic change inside university-cost.component.ts itself, but shared-constant changes carry regression risk for Cost & Financial Aid's Budget Fit indicator and currency toggle if the two consumers' lookups diverge.
Discovery & Research — University Profile TC-UPROF-076 uni-recommended-courses.component.html adds truncate + nz-tooltip to the course name paragraph. The existing 'Recommended Courses panel' TC should verify long course names truncate correctly and the full name shows on hover instead of wrapping/overflowing.
New Surfaces
Discovery & Research — University Profile — Fit Overview's budget line ('Your budget: Up to X') now converts the student's USD-denominated budget cap into the university's local currency via CurrencyConversionService.convert(), falls back to the raw USD amount when conversion is unavailable, and re-renders automatically once currency rates finish loading (ratesLoaded$ subscription). This currency-aware budget/tuition pairing is not covered by any existing TC-UPROF item (CC-31709).
